Research

Active projects with published or forthcoming results.

Regime Switching in Political Risk

Research Assistant · Sep 2024–Present

Replicated a state-of-the-art regime-switching paper and produced new results on political risk ratings data. Developed jump models with XGBoost classifiers to identify regime switching in means and slopes, achieving out-of-sample recall rates of 0.6–0.9. Extended abstract accepted at an AI in Business conference. A publishable paper (ABS 3+) is in preparation.¹

Mapping the Landscape of Franchising Research

Research Assistant · Sep 2024–Present

Applied LDA and BERT topic modeling to a corpus of 555 ISoF conference papers (2003–2024). Conducted preprocessing, semantic validation through expert review, and prepared visualizations and manuscript. Paper presented at ISoF 2025 in Cyprus, where it received the Best Student Paper Award for methodological rigor and contributions to franchising research.²
